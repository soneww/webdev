The Assignment

Welcome to our responsive website design assignment! We understand the importance of creating a website that looks great and functions well on any device, whether it's a desktop computer, a tablet, or a smartphone. Our goal with this assignment is to help you develop the skills and knowledge you need to create websites that are not only visually appealing, but also easy to use and navigate on any screen size. We'll cover topics like user experience design, responsive design principles, and best practices for web development. As you work on your assignment, you'll have the opportunity to explore different design tools and techniques, experiment with different layouts and styles, and get feedback from your peers and instructors. By the end of the assignment, you'll have a solid understanding of how to create websites that are responsive, accessible, and user-friendly. We're excited to see what you'll create and look forward to supporting you throughout the process. Let's get started!
